ABOUT US

Café Prague brings you a European twist to your favorite Californian dishes. Our specialities include Goulash paprika with dumplings, Roast Duck with red cabbage and dumplings, as well as a rich variety of organic mixed greens, including salad Nicoise, and Caeser salad.

We strive to deliver upmost quality to our clients, so we are very conscious of where we get our ingredients from. We work with only the best suppliers, including but not limited to Clover stornetta, Acme, Semifredi, Alvarado rd. & Liguria Bakery, Niemann Ranch, & Rainbow Groceries.
